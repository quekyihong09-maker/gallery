Cats Type

Explore popular pet cat breeds from around the world.

Persian

Known for their long, silky coats and calm temperament, Persians are one of the most popular lap cats. They thrive in quiet, indoor environments and love gentle companionship.

Siamese

Siamese cats are highly vocal and social, forming strong bonds with their owners. Their striking blue eyes and colour-pointed fur make them instantly recognisable.

Maine Coon

One of the largest domestic cat breeds, Maine Coons are friendly, playful, and intelligent. Their thick, shaggy coats were made for cold climates and outdoor adventures.

Scottish Fold

Famous for their unique folded ears and round faces, Scottish Folds are gentle and adaptable. They get along well with children and other pets, making them ideal family cats.
